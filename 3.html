HOW TO MAKE FRUIT SALAD Prepare the fruit: wash (and dry) the strawberries, grapes, and blueberries, and peel the pineapple, oranges, and mangoes.

Then slice everything up into bite-sized pieces and place in a large mixing bowl.

Make the dressing: whisk the lime zest, lime juice, and honey together in a small bowl.

Toss the salad: drizzle the honey lime dressing on the fruit salad and gently toss it all together. Voila fruit salad!
